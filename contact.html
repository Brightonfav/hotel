Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we offer both shared and private airport transfers. Shared transfers are complimentary for stays of 3 nights or more. Private transfers can be arranged at an additional cost. Please contact our concierge at least 48 hours before your arrival to arrange.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Breakfast is included in most of our room packages. You can check if breakfast is included in your specific booking during the reservation process. We offer both continental and full breakfast options at our restaurant from 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM daily.

Do you have facilities for business meetings?

Yes, our hotel features several fully-equipped meeting rooms and conference facilities. We can accommodate small business meetings up to large conferences. Our business center offers services such as printing, copying, and high-speed internet. Contact our events team for more information.

Is there parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer both self-parking and valet parking options. Self-parking is available at $25 per night, while valet parking is $35 per night. Both options include unlimited in and out privileges during your stay. Electric vehicle charging stations are also available.

What amenities does the hotel offer?

Our hotel offers a range of amenities including a swimming pool, fitness center, spa, restaurants, bar/lounge, business center, concierge service, room service, and free high-speed WiFi throughout the property. We also provide laundry services, luggage storage, and 24-hour front desk assistance.
